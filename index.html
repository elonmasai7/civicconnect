Healthcare Reform Act (H.R. 2378)

Introduced: March 15, 2023 Sponsor: Rep. Jane Smith Status: In Committee

Overview

This bill aims to make healthcare more affordable for everyone. It will lower costs for families and improve access to doctors.

Key Provisions

  • Caps prescription drug costs at $35/month
  • Expands free preventative care services
  • Provides subsidies to help lower-income families afford insurance

Projected Impact

Cost Savings

Average family saves $1,200/year on healthcare

Coverage Expansion

4 million more Americans would gain coverage

Drug Affordability

90% of seniors would see lower prescription costs
